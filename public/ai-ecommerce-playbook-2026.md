## Chapter 5: Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Over-automating Too Fast

Start with one workflow. Master it. Then expand.

Trying to automate everything at once leads to:
- Broken customer experiences
- Data inconsistencies
- Debugging nightmares

### Mistake 2: Ignoring the Human Touch

Customers know when they're talking to a bot. That's okay â€” most prefer instant bot responses to slow human ones.

But some moments need a human:
- Complaints about quality
- Large orders
- Returning customers with complex needs

Build escalation paths into every automated workflow.

### Mistake 3: Chasing Shiny Tools

New AI tools launch daily. Most are noise.

Stick with:
- Proven LLMs (Claude, GPT-4)
- Established automation platforms (Zapier, Make)
- Simple, reliable infrastructure

The best tool is the one that works, not the newest one.

### Mistake 4: Forgetting About Cash Flow

Automation reduces costs. It doesn't eliminate them.

You still need:
- Initial inventory investment
- Ad spend to acquire customers
- Buffer for unexpected expenses

Don't scale marketing faster than your cash flow supports.
